openp2pdesign.org

Design for Open Systems, Processes, Projects, Places

Started in 2007, openp2pdesign.org documents research on open, collaborative, and co-design projects and the systems that enable them. A personal website of Massimo Menichinelli , Doctor of Arts in New Media & Master of Science in Industrial Design, contains his research and publications (including several books) on Open Design, FabLab and User-driven Open and Social Innovation initiatives.

The starting point for this research was Open P2P Design , a set of design guidelines and concepts for supporting communities in developing open and peer-to-peer collaborative activities. Its evolution, OpenMetaDesign is a framework for the meta-design of open, collaborative and distributed processes.
