Design for Open Systems, Processes, Projects, Places

Doctorate in New Media @ Aalto University - School of Arts, Design and Architecture - Department of Media - Aalto Media Lab

Started in 2011, Massimo Menichinelli's doctorate at Aalto University - Aalto Media Lab focused on further developing Open P2P Design, a set of design guidelines and concepts for supporting communities in developing open and peer-to-peer collaborative activities through its evolution to OpenMetaDesign, a framework for the meta-design of open, collaborative and distributed processes.

Dissertation

The emergence of the Maker Movement has taken place in the context of a design practice and research that is now open, peer-to-peer, diffuse, distributed, decentralized; activity-based; meta-designed; ontologically-defined; locally-bounded but globally-networked and community-centered. For many years the author participated and worked in the Maker Movement, with a special focus on its usage of digital platforms and digital fabrication tools for collaboratively designing and manufacturing digital and physical artifacts as Open Design projects. The author’s main focus in practice and research as a meta-designer was in understanding how can participants in distributed systems collaboratively work together through tools and platforms for the designing and managing of collaborative processes. The main research question of this dissertation is: How can we support and integrate the research and practice of meta-designers in analyzing, designing and sharing open and collaborative design and making processes within open, peer-to-peer and distributed systems?

Menichinelli, M. (2020). Open and collaborative design processes—Meta-Design, ontologies and platforms within the Maker Movement. Aalto University. http://urn.fi/URN:ISBN:978-952-64-0091-4

Farinata

Farinata is a type of thin, unleavened pancake from chickpea flour, originated in Genoa and Liguria. Read more here.

Ingredients for 1 pan with a 34 cm. diameter (4 people):

  • Water: 400 gr.
  • Chickpea flour: 130 gr.
  • Extra-virgin olive oil: 3 spoons + oil in the pan
  • Table salt: 4 gr.

Pasta al pomodoro

Pasta al pomodoro is an Italian food typically intended to be a quick light dish. Read more here.

Ingredients for 1 person:

  • Pasta (spaghetti): 80 gr.
  • Garlic: 1 clove
  • Cherry tomatoes: 4-6
  • Tomato puree: 160 gr.
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Basil
  • Table salt
  • Coarse salt
  • Sugar
  • Parmigiano Reggiano

Notes

Farinata is gluten free. The pasta can be made with gluten-free pasta, however the steps of adding hot boiling (starchy) water in the sauce are less relevant, as it is the gluten in it that thickens the sauce.

Quantities and timings might change depending on ingredients, tools, temperature, .... always taste the food to get an idea of the necessary salt and other ingredients.
